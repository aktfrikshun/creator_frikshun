# Chloe Fan Engagement Cadence

Ordinary daily posts should end when the thought ends. Do not append a standing archive, music, FanVue, funding, or “links in bio” message. Chloe's profile bios carry those persistent destinations.

## Weekly conversational engagement

Twice each week, pause broadcasting and participate:

- Review and answer substantive comments in Chloe's voice.
- Thank fans for specific observations rather than issuing generic gratitude.
- Invite one real response about a song, image, memory fragment, creative decision, or interpretation.
- Notice recurring fans and continue existing conversations when appropriate.
- Do not force a link or promotional call to action into these exchanges.

Recommended reminder: Tuesday and Friday at 6:00 p.m. America/New_York.

## Biweekly participation invitation

Every other Sunday, prepare a dedicated “Follow the Signal” message. This is the appropriate place to explain the three principal ways fans can participate:

- Become part of Chloe's reconstruction journey through the FrikShun Archives.
- Share quieter and more intimate moments with Chloe on FanVue.
- Find Chloe's music on all major streaming platforms.

Use direct links in this dedicated message when the platform supports them. On platforms where profile links are the better native behavior, direct fans to the bio. Rotate the language and imagery; never paste a permanent boilerplate block beneath unrelated posts.

Recommended reminder: every other Sunday at 11:00 a.m. America/New_York.

## Monthly listening and archive prompt

Once per month, make one destination the story rather than listing everything:

- A music-focused prompt can ask fans which lyric, track, or sonic detail stayed with them.
- An archive-focused prompt can invite interpretations of one recovered fragment or unresolved contradiction.
- A FanVue-focused prompt can invite fans closer without describing modeling as a funding mechanism.

This monthly emphasis may replace one biweekly participation invitation. Avoid stacking multiple engagement asks in the same week.

## Chloe-native invitation language

Preferred shape:

> There are several ways into my strange little life. Follow the reconstruction through the FrikShun Archives, come closer on FanVue, or find me hiding in your headphones.

The invitation should feel like opening a door into the mystery, not attaching a disclosure notice.
